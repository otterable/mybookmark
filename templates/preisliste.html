About the Stimmungskompass:

Stimmungskompass" is a web app designed to gauge and enhance the public's connection with their town. Its primary function is to gather residents' opinions and feelings about their urban environment.

Users, after creating an account, can share insights about their preferred locations, leisure spots, and relaxation areas within the city. Additionally, they can report on practical issues such as parking difficulties or areas where they feel unsafe.

The Stimmungskompass is centered around participatory planning. Users have the opportunity to propose projects aimed at improving the city's living quality. These could range from infrastructural changes like new bicycle paths (Radweg) to community-centric initiatives.

Other members can then engage with these suggestions through voting and commenting, fostering a collaborative environment. This feature is designed not only to collect ideas but also to assist city authorities in identifying and prioritizing projects that resonate most with the residents.

By bridging the gap between the town's administration and its inhabitants, Stimmungskompass strives to create a more cohesive community, where the collective goal is to continually enhance the living quality of the city. It's a platform where the voices of the people are heard, and their ideas are valued and potentially brought to fruition.
